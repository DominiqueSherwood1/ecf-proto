Register or sign in to the Early Career Framework induction programme

The Early Career Framework induction programme is the new statutory requirement for the induction of early career teachers that applies from September 2021 to all maintained schools in England.

There is no cost to schools for this training, it is funded by the Department for Education.

! Warning You should be responsible for managing inductions, or a senior leader, to register the school.

If you already have an account you can sign in here.

Start now

Before you start

You will need to be able to answer an email sent to your school-based email address.

If you have already arranged which provider from the DFE Early Career Framework approved partners you will work with in 2021 to 2022 you will be able to confirm that on this service. You can then set up your school ready to start your induction programme in September.
